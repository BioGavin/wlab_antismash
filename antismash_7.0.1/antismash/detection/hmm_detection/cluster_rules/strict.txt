DEFINE ANY_KS AS (PKS_KS or ene_KS or mod_KS or hyb_KS or itr_KS or tra_KS)

RULE T1PKS
    CATEGORY PKS
    DESCRIPTION Type I polyketide
    CUTOFF 20
    NEIGHBOURHOOD 20
    CONDITIONS cds(PKS_AT and ANY_KS)
